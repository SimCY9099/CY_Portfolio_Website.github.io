Steam

Introduction

The Steam Game Dataset project focuses on analyzing the vast collection of video games available on the Steam platform. The objective is to gain insights into the latest released games, understand player preferences, and identify popular gaming genres. By leveraging the dataset's comprehensive information, we can provide valuable analysis and recommendations to both gamers and industry professionals.

Data

Dataset Description: The Steam game dataset consists of approximately 60,000 video games available on the Steam platform. It includes various attributes such as game names, release years, ratings from Metacritic and reviewers, positivity ratios, and gameplay completion times. The dataset provides a rich source of information for understanding game quality, user ratings, and other factors influencing player satisfaction.

Data Preprocessing: To ensure accurate analysis, the dataset underwent preprocessing steps. This involved cleaning the data, handling missing values, and enhancing the dataset with additional columns for easier analysis, such as grouping games by major genres or types. Any missing values related to ratings were replaced with "NotRated" to maintain consistency and indicate the absence of a rating.

Conclusion

Through this project, we delve into the Steam game dataset to uncover trends, evaluate game ratings, analyze positivity ratios, and identify top-performing genres. The analysis aims to provide valuable insights into the gaming industry and help gamers and industry professionals make informed decisions. By leveraging the power of data analysis, we can gain a deeper understanding of the gaming landscape and contribute to the growth and development of the industry.
